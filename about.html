Background

A practitioner who has been in the room where it gets built

Loic brings a practitioner's perspective to AI advisory shaped by years of building, deploying, and operating AI-enabled products across SaaS, analytics, digital identity, and edge environments.

The difference between a strategy deck and real change is execution. His engagements focus on what happens after the whiteboard: the workflow redesign, the change management, the governance structures, and the metrics that tell you whether it's actually working.

He has led advisory engagements for organizations seeking practical, business-aligned approaches to AI adoption across operations, customer workflows, product strategy, and service delivery helping leadership teams move beyond experimentation by identifying high-value use cases, assessing organizational readiness, and translating AI opportunity into executable operating plans.
